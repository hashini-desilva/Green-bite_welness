Why Calculate Your BMR & TDEE?

Understanding your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) helps you know how much energy your body needs at rest and during daily activities.

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Adjust calories for weight loss or gain
  • Balance your nutrition & lifestyle
  • Improve overall health awareness

💡 Small lifestyle changes can make a big difference in your wellbeing. Use the calculator below to discover your numbers and take control of your health!
